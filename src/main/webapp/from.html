About Shipping in India

India boasts one of the largest merchant shipping fleets among developing countries. With 13 major ports and over 200 minor ports, marine travel and cargo transportation is thriving like never before. Whether it is luxury coastal cruises, budget-friendly ferries, or cargo liners, India is coastline offers it all.

The government is actively promoting **sustainable and digital marine services**, and our platform aligns with that mission to make ticket booking effortless, secure, and transparent.

Experience affordable, comfortable journeys across the country via government and private ferry operators.

With rising demand for marine-based connectivity and green logistics, India is steadily turning its vast coastline into a channel of opportunity — connecting cities, islands, and economies. Whether you're commuting, exploring, or transporting goods, the sea is now more accessible than ever.

From the pristine waters of Lakshadweep to the buzzing docks of Mumbai, every voyage tells a story. And we’re here to make yours smooth, scenic, and satisfying.

What is Marine Reservation?

Marine reservation is your digital gateway to booking seamless ferry, cruise, and sea travel experiences across India’s vast coastline. From majestic cruises across the Arabian Sea to short inter-island ferry rides in Andaman, our platform empowers citizens to explore the sea safely, affordably, and with confidence.

Built to align with India’s Digital Maritime Mission, the reservation system integrates public-private marine operators, digital tickets, real-time schedules, and upcoming smart cruise terminals.
